GelTrace

GelTrace is a food production workflow system designed to integrate with the DEAR inventory management system. It enables full inventory traceability from raw ingredient to shipped product and presents a straightforward Android tablet app to be used by kitchen staff.

Behind the scenes, GelTrace drives a pipeline of assemblies for each stage of the production process: from raw ingredients to optional intermediate products (such as bases & jams), to final bulk product and its corresponding packaged & boxed products. It is batch number/expiry date aware and handles wastage & the imprecise nature of filling tubs by hand.
